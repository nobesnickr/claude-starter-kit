# Decision Registry

A living record of significant decisions for this project. Add an entry whenever you make a choice that would be expensive to reverse or that a future reader would wonder about.

## ADR-001: [Short title of the decision]
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Status:** Accepted
- **Context:** What prompted this? What problem or constraint?
- **Decision:** What did we decide?
- **Why:** The reasoning, and the main alternative we did not pick.
- **Consequences:** What this makes easier, and what it makes harder.
